# **App Name**: Sparkle Pro

## Core Features:

- Quote & Booking Engine: A multi-step form allowing users to input home details, desired cleaning frequency, and contact information to generate a cleaning quote. This data will be stored in Firestore for lead management.
- Personalized Cleaning Plan Tool: An AI-powered tool that suggests a custom cleaning plan and checklist based on user-provided home characteristics and specific cleaning preferences, utilizing the detailed '49-Point Checklist' as its knowledge base.
- Interactive Service Showcase: Displays the '49-Point Checklist' in an interactive format and details service offerings via cards (Recurring Cleaning, One-Time Deep Cleans, Moving In/Out services).
- Trust & Social Proof Display: Showcases icons highlighting key advantages like 'Bonded & Insured,' 'Eco-Friendly Products,' 'Background Checked PROs,' and a '24-Hour Re-clean Guarantee,' alongside a slider for customer testimonials.
- Lead Notification System: Automated system (e.g., via Cloud Functions) to trigger email or SMS notifications to company administrators or relevant franchisees upon the submission of a new quote request.
- Informational & Recruitment Pages: Dedicated sections for 'About Us' providing company history and values, and a 'Franchise/Careers' area for business opportunities and job applications.
- Comprehensive Footer Navigation: Provides essential links to FAQs, Gift Cards, a Blog, and social media channels for additional information and engagement.
- Application Hosting: Utilize Firebase Hosting for secure and reliable deployment of the website with custom domain support.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A clean, inviting blue-teal (#44A3BF) that evokes professionalism and freshness.
- Background color: An airy, subtle off-white with a hint of blue-teal (#F0F6F7), maintaining a clean and spacious feel.
- Accent color: A vibrant, fresh aqua-green (#5FFFB3) to draw attention to call-to-actions and key elements, complementing the primary color.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for its friendly, professional, and contemporary geometric appearance.
- Utilize modern, clean line icons to represent services and trust elements, ensuring visual consistency with the 'airy' design.
- Employ a responsive, grid-based layout with ample white space, sticky navigation in the header, large high-quality hero imagery, and distinct section breaks for clear content hierarchy.
- Subtle and smooth transitions or hover effects for interactive elements like service cards, buttons, and testimonials sliders, enhancing user engagement without being distracting.
